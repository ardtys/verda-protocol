mission

Why We Exist

For decades, complexity in global supply chains has been weaponized to hide fraud, cut corners, and externalize risk onto consumers. From fake organic labels to adulterated olive oil, from mislabeled seafood to compromised cold chains — the system rewards opacity.

We're building the infrastructure to change that. Every product should have a verifiable story. Every consumer should have access to the truth. Every honest producer should be able to prove it.

Our Vision

A world where every food product has a verifiable, transparent history.

Our Mission

Make supply chain transparency the default, not the exception.

Our Impact

Protecting consumers and empowering honest producers worldwide.

Food Fraud

$40B+ annually. Olive oil cut with cheaper oils. Honey adulterated with corn syrup. "Organic" labels on conventional produce. "Wild-caught" on farmed fish. Systemic deception at industrial scale.

Cold Chain Failures

$35B in annual losses from temperature excursions. Vaccines, seafood, dairy — they all need controlled temperatures. Most failures go undetected until someone gets sick.

Recall Nightmares

Average food recall takes 2-3 weeks. By then, contaminated products are consumed. Without granular tracing, entire batches are destroyed — massive waste, incomplete protection.
