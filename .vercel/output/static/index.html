about

We're solving a $40 billion food fraud problem that affects every person who eats. From fake organic labels to adulterated olive oil, from mislabeled seafood to compromised cold chains — the global food system was designed for opacity.

Verda makes transparency the default. Our protocol combines IoT sensors, blockchain verification, and simple QR scanning to create an immutable record of every product's journey from farm to shelf.

Our mission: Every product should have a verifiable story. Every consumer should have access to the truth.

how it works

From Seed to Shelf to Scan

01. Farm Registration

Farmer logs crop, location, and inputs via mobile app. Works offline in remote areas.

Mobile App Offline-first

02. Harvest & Handoff

NFC scan records custody transfer. Temperature logger activated. Multi-party signature required.

NFC IoT Sensors

03. Processing

Factory logs transformation events. QR code generated and printed on packaging.

QR Generation Batch Tracking

04. Cold Chain Transit

Continuous monitoring: temperature, humidity, GPS. Real-time alerts on excursions.

GPS Temperature Alerts

05. Retail Arrival

Receiving scan verifies cold chain integrity. Status badge assigned to product.

Verification Status Badge

06. Consumer Scan

Any smartphone. Any browser. Full journey revealed in 2 seconds. No app download.

Web-based Instant

07. On-Chain Proof

Merkle batch anchored to Blockchain every 60 seconds. Cryptographic verification available.

Blockchain Merkle Proofs
