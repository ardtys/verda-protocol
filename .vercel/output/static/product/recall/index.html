the problem

Why Recalls Are Broken

Weeks to Execute

Average food recall takes 2-3 weeks from detection to completion. By then, contaminated products are consumed, distributed further, or forgotten in consumers' refrigerators.

Massive Over-Recall

Without granular tracing, companies recall entire production runs. A contamination affecting 100 units leads to destroying 100,000. Billions in unnecessary waste annually.

Consumer Notification Fails

Press releases and store notices reach maybe 20% of affected consumers. The rest never know they have recalled products at home.

Liability Limbo

Without precise records, it's impossible to prove where contamination occurred. Everyone blames everyone else. Legal battles drag on for years.
