Sensor Specifications

VRD-T100 Temperature Logger

  • Range: -40°C to +85°C
  • Accuracy: ±0.2°C
  • Battery: 2 years (readings every 60s)
  • Size: 45mm × 45mm × 12mm
  • IP67 water resistant
  • FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliant

Ideal for: Vaccines, dairy, meat, seafood

VRD-TH200 Temp + Humidity

  • Temp: -40°C to +85°C (±0.2°C)
  • Humidity: 0-100% RH (±2%)
  • Battery: 18 months
  • Size: 50mm × 50mm × 15mm
  • IP67 water resistant
  • Shock detection (3-axis accelerometer)

Ideal for: Produce, pharmaceuticals, flowers

VRD-GPS300 Location Tracker

  • GPS + GLONASS + Galileo
  • Accuracy: 2.5m CEP
  • Battery: 6 months (hourly updates)
  • Geofencing support
  • Motion-activated tracking
  • LTE-M / NB-IoT connectivity

Ideal for: High-value shipments, international transit

VRD-GATE100 Gateway

  • LoRaWAN 1.0.3 compatible
  • Range: 2km urban, 10km rural
  • Supports 1000+ sensors
  • 4G LTE backhaul
  • 12V or solar powered
  • Operating temp: -20°C to +60°C

Ideal for: Warehouses, trucks, distribution centers
